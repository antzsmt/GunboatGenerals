    • General Tips

    Operations
    Basic Strategy
  • Read the base notes. If you disagree or have a better idea, check into chat and bounce the ideas off the leaders.
  • Clear a path to the core, where there is an open area for zookas to sit and tear it down.
  • Along this path, use your GBE (artillery and barrage) on groups of turrets, ones where you can hit 4, 3 or at least 2 turrets with one shot.
  • Do not retreat.
  • Intel is a precious resource. Often it is the limiting factor on starting ops at our skill level. As such, do not waste intel on sabotage.
  • Don't use medics -- they won't do much against high level turret damage, especially on higher level ops.
    • Advanced Strategies
  • When using artillery, hit two or more buildings at once (zoom in so you don't misfire).
  • Use your supporting GBE (health, shocks) early. Earlier in the attack, more troops will be alive, which does more damage than giving health to the last two heavies.
  • Use various types of GBE, as you use the same one, it becomes more expensive, so see how you can combine types to minimize total cost (smoke / shocks, health / shocks, artillery / barrage).
  • Destroy a lot of power cells and use the energy to attack main defenses with GBE only if you're a high level (your artillery and barrages will do more damage than lower levels).
  • If you're attacking the core with hooka, and there's only a rocket launcher in range of the zookas, shock the rockets after the heavies are dead. This is because the heavies soak up damage from the other turrets that can't kill the zookas as fast. The rockets will kill the zookas very fast due to splash damage.
  • Use grenadiers only for well positioned canons, machine guns, and flame throwers.
    • Home Base
    Basic Strategy
  • Focus on offense, only red and purple statues. Do not upgrade defenses until you're a level 50 or so, because the only people that will attack are those who are confident they'll win, which doesn't gain as much for you or the Task Force. Also it's more fun to attack than watch attacks!
    • Advanced Strategies
  • When doing armory upgrades, to get more gold, change troop type and then cancel the creation of the troops (you'll get 200k more gold or so from freeing up those resources).
  • When upgrading landing craft, maximize the time-value of resources. Do not upgrade two landing craft to an "in-between" level. That is, do not upgrade two landing craft to capacities of 17 or 19. Only upgrade to 17 if you intend to upgrade to 18 (and can actually hold more units) soon thereafter.
